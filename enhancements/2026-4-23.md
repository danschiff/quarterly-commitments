### Mark Initiatives that have quarterly commitments
check the Committed Quarter field (customfield_11245) and for initiatives committed to the same quarter: a) prioritize those at the top of each team's report and b) mark the initiative in a way that makes it clear that it's committed for the same quarter

### Exclude slipping items for rolling commitments
Check for a Tag "Rolling" and if the Epic is tagged with that, instead of marking it as "Slipping" mark it as "Rolling Commitment". Exclude it from the slack messages as they don't need attention from the manager

### Add Health status from Jira
For each initiative and Epic, include the Health status field from Jira: customfield_10883
